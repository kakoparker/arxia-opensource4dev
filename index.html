sec. 03 — case study

Case Study: TYPO3 as Rwanda's Digital Public Good

Problem: The Government of Rwanda was looking to remake all of the web portals of the government using a single framework in a centralized and sustainable way.

Our Solution: We engaged with the local government to propose the OpenSource4Development approach. We partnered with their local ecosystem, trained local companies in the TYPO3 CMS technology and framework, kickstarted the local TYPO3 Community, and co-built all of the government websites with local professionals.

Results: We didn't just build websites—we standardized the digital presence for an entire nation. This flagship project demonstrates our methodology's power to create lasting, transformative change at national scale.
